Cake

Soft, Delicious Sponge Cake

Description

This lovely sponge cake is easy to make and uses the ingredients you have in your cupboard.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup, All-purpose Flour
  • 1 TBSP Baking Powder
  • 1 TBSP Hot water
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whip eggs until foamy
  2. Preheat oven and line baking tin
  3. Sift dry ingredients
  4. Mix Liquids
  5. Fold dry ingredients into whipped eggs
  6. Fold liquids into dough
  7. Bake for 35minutes
  8. Remove and let rest
